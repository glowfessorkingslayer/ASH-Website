Mission

Our club is dedicated to promoting the scientific world view in society and especially on campus. Join us! We are a bunch of fun-loving students who care about the world.   

Our mission is to provide social, intellectual, and advocacy needs for atheists, agnostics, seculars, brights, eupraxsophers, freethinkers, humanists, skeptics, and the non-religious at the University of Texas at Dallas.
